# User Story: 04 - Define Data Retention and Lifecycle Policies

**As a** Compliance Manager,
**I want** to establish data retention and lifecycle policies for all data in the NAPTA system,
**so that** we meet regulatory requirements, minimize compliance risks, and manage storage costs effectively.

## Acceptance Criteria

* Retention periods are defined for each data classification level (INTERNAL, CONFIDENTIAL, FINANCIAL, AUDIT, MIXED)
* Automated lifecycle policies are configured to archive or delete data after retention period expires
* Data lifecycle events are logged and auditable
* Administrators can view and modify retention policies
* System prevents manual deletion of data subject to retention requirements
* Reports are generated showing data lifecycle compliance status
* Legal and compliance teams approve retention policies before activation

## Notes

* Different data types may have different retention requirements based on industry regulations
* This helps mitigate compliance violations and associated penalties
* Lifecycle policies should work with audit logging to maintain compliance trails
* Storage optimization is a secondary benefit of proper data lifecycle management
